What can I tell you about me?

Hey there! I'm Prathmesh Mangale. I began capturing beautiful moments with my camera in 2018, and my passion has only grown stronger since. Initially, I did a lot of free shoots, asked my friends to be my subjects and even went to click pictures for their family and connections. It helped me build valuable contacts and gradually led to numerous work opportunities. I've had the privilege of working for some of the biggest brands, as reflected in my profile. My clients include renowned companies like Samsung, Tata, and Swiggy, as well as prominent malls such as Phoenix Marketcity and Infinity Mall.

In the food industry, I've worked for McDonald's and Jimi's Burger, and in real estate, I've had the pleasure of collaborating with esteemed clients like Hiranandani.

I entered the field of photography because of my deep passion for creativity, my eye for detail and my drive for perfectionism. I am dedicated to capturing every detail perfectly and making my work stand out. Constantly striving to try new things, I have evolved into a hybrid shooter, always pushing the boundaries of my craft.
